Overview

Research

The purpose of the app is for people of all backgrounds and ages can learn something new. Where they can use on the go for a quick study session within their busy schedule. To get started, I did a competitive analysis on 3 similar popular apps and how they accomplish their goals. Keeping in mind these following questions:

  • “Why do I think this is a successful product (or not) in terms of meeting the goals of the users?”
  • “What do I think the designers did to make the product successful (or not), in terms of the users?”
  • “How do I feel about using the product? How would I make it better?”

To add to this research, I needed to know firsthand how people feel about using vocabulary apps. I interviewed 3 candidates and summarized the results into what they were doing, thinking, and feeling. From them, Oliver was born.
